Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

Time Letters ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

Our Core Privacy Promise

Your journal entries and personal reflections are stored exclusively on your device. We never access, transmit, or share your personal data.

Data Collection and Storage

What We DON'T Collect

Time Letters is designed to be a fully local application. We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Your journal entries or personal reflections
  • Your custom personas or time capsules
  • Your name, email address, or any account information (in local-only mode)
  • Your device identifiers or location data
  • Your usage patterns or analytics data
  • Any personal information about you or your journaling habits

What We DO Store (Locally on Your Device)

All data is stored in your device's local storage:

  • Journal Entries: Stored in your browser's localStorage
  • Personas: Your custom avatars and their configurations
  • Preferences: Dark mode settings, display preferences
  • Usage Data: Export counts, streak tracking (all locally)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

Third-Party Services

GitHub Pages (Privacy Policy Hosting)

This privacy policy is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect:

  • Visitor IP addresses
  • Basic access logs

See GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

App Stores

When you download Time Letters from Google Play Store or Apple App Store:

  • Standard app analytics (download counts, crashes) are collected by the store
  • This is standard for all apps and is not shared with us

Data Security

  • All local data is stored in your device's secure storage
  • Cloud-synced data (if enabled) is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We implement industry-standard security measures

You have complete control over your data:

Children's Privacy
